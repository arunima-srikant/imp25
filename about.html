About Us

We’re a bunch of fourth-year undergraduate students who have built an output to help café entrepreneurs elevate their presence, and thrive across both physical and digital spaces. Afterall, every person deserves to tell their story! Think of this portal as a cosy café you can step into anytime. Inside, you’ll find a visual brand book designed as an interactive café simulation. From the menu design to where your logo will best garner attention, you can hover over every branding collateral to visualise and understand how to navigate marketing decisions. It’s simple, visually engaging, and fun to explore. And because every café owner deserves a friendly advisor, we’ve added a chatbot that speaks your language. It’s backed by research but speaks in practical, easy-to-apply advice helping you untangle marketing questions without the overwhelm. Whether you’re opening your first cafe or looking to rebrand, ByteSize is here to help you fuel your creativity, sharpen your vision and curate a brand that grows with you.
